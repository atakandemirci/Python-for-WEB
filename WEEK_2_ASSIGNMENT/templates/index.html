ABOUT ME

Hello Friends

My name is Atakan Demirci. I’m an equity analyst. Not sure how familiar you are with the finance industry but that basically means my job is to analyze publicly traded stocks and publish reports about them.

I am starting an MS in Finance at Fordham in the fall and right now I am taking coding classes at Baruch. I am planning to take the CFA examinations starting in the summer. I’ve also started an economics and finance blog because I wanted to explain financial topics in an easy way and help people with their financial literacy.

After graduation, my short-term goal is to stay in New York and work at an investment bank like Goldman Sachs, JP Morgan, or Morgan Stanley. That way I can improve my skills and my experience with emerging markets. And in the long-term I hope to become a portfolio manager and keep growing my clients’ assets.

In terms of professional experience, I worked on Wall Street, and at the Istanbul Stock Exchange. I have analyzed a wide range of companies. I have built a strong track record averaging 25% return. I helped investors generate new investment ideas and research stocks they already own.

In my experience in the finance industry, I’ve seen how easy it can be to get comfortable and stop innovating. So I made a resolution to always keep improving myself, and to seek analyst positions that could expand my knowledge of global finance.
